My Random Skill

A random skill that I have been blessed with is having a great sense of direction. I always am very aware of where I am and know how to get places. often I struggle with street names but if someone asked me to go to a specific place I could get there no problem.

I believe this skill developed as a result of living in the Midwest. I did not have any mountains to guide me so I had to learn to be observant of my surroundings when I would drive and I would pick up details about the city and world around me. It's a bit of a random skill but one that I'm grateful I have.

My View On Growth

Growth is what life is all about. It's something that we never stop doing. Oftentimes we may feel like we're not moving forward but if we just pause and look back at how far we've come in comparison to where we were before we can almost always see that we have changed and become better.

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.

Heavenly Father sees divine potential in each one of us and knows we will make mistakes. He sent the Savior for us to grow through him and make up for where we lack. Without the Savior we are nothing but with him we have all of the potential in the world to progress and become like him.
